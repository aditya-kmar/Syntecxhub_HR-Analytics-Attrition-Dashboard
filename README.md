# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Attrition Analysis)

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition and identify key workforce trends. This dashboard helps HR teams understand employee turnover by analyzing factors such as age, gender, education, business travel, marital status, department, and years at the company.

# 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 👨 Gender Analysis
- Male employees contribute a higher share of attrition than female employees.
- Indicates that retention strategies may need to focus more on the male workforce.

### 🎓 Education Field Analysis
- Employees from **Life Sciences** experience the highest attrition.
- Medical and Marketing fields also show noticeable employee turnover.

### 👥 Age Group Analysis
- The **26–35 years** age group has the largest workforce and the highest attrition count.
- Younger professionals appear more likely to switch jobs.

### 💍 Marital Status Analysis
- **Single employees** have the highest attrition.
- Married employees show comparatively better retention.

### ✈ Business Travel Analysis
- Employees who **travel frequently** have significantly higher attrition.
- Suggests frequent travel may impact work-life balance.

### 📅 Years at Company
- Most employees leave within the first few years of joining.
- Attrition decreases as employee tenure increases.

### 💼 Job Role Analysis
- Laboratory Technicians, Sales Executives, and Research Scientists report the highest employee exits.
- These roles may require targeted retention initiatives.
